About Us

Welcome to Feast Heaven, where passion for food meets a commitment to exceptional service. Since our humble beginnings in 1872, we've been on a mission to redefine dining experiences.

Our story started when Tillu Singh, a visionary food enthusiast, embarked on a culinary journey to bring together flavors from around the world. Over the years, Feast Heaven has evolved into a beacon of culinary excellence, renowned for its innovative dishes and warm hospitality.

At Feast Heaven, our talented team of chefs, led by Executive Chef Chota Bheem, brings a wealth of expertise and creativity to the kitchen. Each dish is a masterpiece, meticulously crafted with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients to tantalize your taste buds.

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains unchanged - to provide an unforgettable dining experience that delights our guests and celebrates the artistry of food.
